Research

  Intelligent Transportation System

An intelligent transportation system (ITS) is an advanced application which aims to provide innovative services relating to different modes of transport and traffic management and enable users to be better informed and make safer, more coordinated, and 'smarter' use of transport networks. Our research mainly aimed at ITS, we use machine learning, operation optimization and network system technology to improve traffic operation efficiency and user experience.
